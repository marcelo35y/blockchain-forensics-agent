Detection Algorithms
🔍

TransactionTracer

Multi-hop transaction tracing with taint propagation analysis. Tracks fund flows across addresses, detects circular flows, and identifies mixing/convergence points.

👥

WalletClusterer

Identifies related wallets via co-spending analysis, temporal correlation, and behavioral fingerprinting. Detects Sybil clusters and coordinated manipulation.

⚠️

AnomalyDetector

Statistical anomaly detection using Z-Score, IQR, Isolation Forest, and velocity analysis. Identifies unusual transaction patterns in real-time.

🧩

PatternAnalyzer

Detects money laundering patterns: mixing services, layering chains, peel chains, and round-trip transactions. Behavioral pattern matching engine.

⚖️

ComplianceChecker

OFAC sanctions screening, structuring detection, and FATF Travel Rule compliance. Generates compliance reports with risk assessments.

📋

ReportGenerator

Automated forensic report generation with executive summaries, risk assessments, chain-of-custody documentation, and HTML export.
